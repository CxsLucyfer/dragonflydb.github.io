Information we collect

We may collect two types of data and information from you. In this section, we describe the categories of information which we may collect, and the applicable circumstances under which such collection is performed.

What is personal information?

“Personal information” is individually identifiable information, namely information that identifies an individual or may with reasonable efforts or together with additional information we have access to, enable the identification of an individual, or may be of private or sensitive nature information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person. Identification of an individual also includes association of such individual with a persistent identifier such as a name, an identification number etc.

Personal information does not include information that has been anonymized or aggregated so that it can no longer be used to identify a specific natural person.

We collect Personal Information from you when you use or interact with our Website and Services.

We collect Personal Information from you and any devices (including mobile devices) you use when you use or access our Website and Services, provide us information on a web form or other text field or through correspondence you and we conduct with each other on any channel of communication.

More specifically we collect and use the following categories and types of information in the following respective circumstances:

  1. Personal Information you provide us actively and voluntarily when you use or interact with our Website and Services:
    • Contact information such as full name, email, position, company, and other content you may provide us in your correspondence with us through various channels of communication, including any online forms and text fields available on our Website.
    • Information we are required or authorized by applicable national laws to require Users to provide us in order to authenticate or identify them or to verify the information we have collected or otherwise provide them with our Services.
  2. Personal Information we automatically obtain when you use or interact with our Website and Services:
    • This is the information we obtain through the Website and Services when Users access or interact with the Website, which is derived, learned, or detected as a result of such access and/or interaction. Keep in mind - most devices allow you to control or disable the use of certain collectable information including location services, in the device’s settings menu.
    • We perform such automatic collection through: (i) use of cookies, web beacons, unique identifiers, and similar technologies, with respect to Users; and (ii) unique identifiers you provided us with, such as email, name, and Technical Identifiers (as defined below). Such means of collection allow us to collect information about the pages and screens Users view, the links they click, engagement within the Services, and other actions you take when using our Website and Services. -Please take into consideration that certain portions of such abovementioned information may also be collected from you when the Website is running in the background, i.e. when it has been launched but not used.
      We generally classify this type of automatically collected personal information under the following categories:
  • “User Interface Usage Information”: this refers to information relating to the Users’ interaction with our Website, such as User clicks when using the Website.
  • “Technical Identifiers”: this refers to various unique identifiers that generally only identify a computer, device or browser. For example, IP address (which may also provide general location information).
  • “Technical Information”: this refers to technical information related to a mobile device or computer, such as browser type, device type and model, operating system etc.

For the avoidance of doubt, any non-Personal Information connected or linked to any Personal Information shall be deemed as Personal Information as long as such connection or linkage exists.
